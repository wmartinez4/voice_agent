   "Perfect! Am I speaking with [TU NOMBRE REAL]?"
   → Responder: "Yes" o "That's me"

   "I'm calling about an outstanding balance of [MONTO] dollars that is [DÍAS] days overdue. Were you aware of this?"
   → Responder: "Yes" o "Tell me more"

4. Jess propone plan de pagos (llama a propose_payment_plan):
   "Would you like me to check payment options for you?"
   → Responder: "Yes" o "Sure"

5. Jess presenta el plan:
   "I can offer you 3 monthly payments of [MONTO] dollars. Does that work for you?"
   → Responder: "Yes, that works" o "I agree"

6. Jess confirma y actualiza (llama a update_status):
   "Perfect! I've noted this in your account. You'll receive a confirmation shortly. Have a great day!"
